Need a rack quote? Here’s all we need.

Send as much as you have and we’ll come back with a rack concept and a number. A photo and a couple of dimensions is enough.

  • A print, CAD, or clear photo of the part(s)
  • Part size & weight (esp. the heaviest part)
  • Parts per rack / per shipment, if known
  • Annual volume or number of trips
  • Ship-from and ship-to lanes
  • Returnable or one-way? Stack / nest / fold?
  • Current pain — damage, ergonomics, supplier
  • Timing — current need or upcoming launch
