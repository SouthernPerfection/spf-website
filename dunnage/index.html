Why SPF

One partner for the rack and the dunnage.

Most rack shops sub out the dunnage; most dunnage shops don’t weld steel. We design the rack and the returnable dunnage as one system, under one roof — so nothing fights the part, and there’s one accountable supplier when something needs to change.

FAQ

Dunnage — answered.

What dunnage types do you offer?

Custom foam inserts and protective foam packaging, plus fabric and returnable dunnage, engineered to your part.

Reusable versus expendable dunnage?

We build durable, reusable and returnable dunnage designed to ride the loop, not one-way packaging.

Do you design dunnage to our part?

Yes. We cut and shape dunnage to your part geometry so surfaces arrive clean, trip after trip.

Can you bundle dunnage with racks?

Yes. We design the rack and the dunnage together as one system, under one roof.

Parts arriving damaged?

Send a part or a print — we’ll design dunnage that protects it.
