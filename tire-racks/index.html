Tire & wheel racks

What are returnable tire racks?

Returnable tire racks are steel racks and carts engineered to move and store tires and wheels through a manufacturing or distribution loop — protecting the tread and bead while shipping dense. Industrial tire storage racks hold tires securely for transport between plants, warehouses, and assembly lines, then return empty for the next load.

SPF builds custom tire and wheel racks and carts to your tire size, stack count, and handling method — stackable, collapsible, or on casters for line-side delivery. Built to print and finished in-house, they protect the product and cube out the trailer.

We build tire and wheel handling for automotive assembly and distribution and logistics operations, alongside our returnable racks and industrial carts.

FAQ

Tire racks — answered.

How many tires or wheels fit per rack?

We design tire and wheel racks for maximum density to your tire size and trailer, balancing part count with safe handling. Send your spec and volume.

Do tire racks protect finished wheels?

Yes. For finished or alloy wheels we add soft-contact protection so faces and finishes aren't marred in transit.

Do you build racks for tires and wheels?

Yes — custom steel tire racks, wheel racks, and carts engineered to your tire or wheel size, stack count, and handling method, built to print.

Are your tire racks returnable and stackable?

Yes. We build stackable and collapsible tire racks designed for the returnable loop — stack loaded, fold or nest empty to cut return freight.

Can you build tire carts for line-side delivery?

Yes. We build ergonomic tire and wheel carts on casters that present product safely at the point of use.
