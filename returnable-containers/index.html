Industrial metal containers

What are industrial metal containers?

Industrial metal containers — including wire mesh containers, collapsible steel containers, and custom steel containers — are reusable steel bins and boxes that store and ship parts through a returnable loop. Stronger and longer-lasting than corrugated or plastic, they protect parts and stack safely.

SPF builds custom returnable containers to your part, load, and stacking — rigid or collapsible, mesh or solid — engineered to fold flat for return freight and set up fast at the line.

We build metal containers for automotive, distribution and logistics, and general manufacturing — with steel pallets and returnable racks to match.

FAQ

Returnable steel containers — answered.

Do you build collapsible steel containers?

Yes. We build collapsible and knock-down steel containers that fold flat for efficient return freight, then set up fast at the line.

Can you make wire mesh containers to our spec?

Yes. We fabricate custom wire mesh and steel containers to your part, load, and stacking requirements, built to print.

Are the containers stackable and returnable?

Yes. Our containers are engineered to stack and to run as returnable assets for many trips.

What is the difference between metal containers and cardboard boxes?

Metal containers are reusable and far stronger — they protect parts better, stack safely, and run for years in a returnable loop, where cardboard is used once and offers little protection.

How do collapsible containers reduce return freight?

Collapsible steel containers fold or knock down flat when empty — often several to one — so you ship far fewer trailers of empties back home.

Can you build containers to fit our racks and pallets?

Yes. We design containers to nest with your steel pallets and racks so the whole returnable system works together.
