FAQ

Automotive returnable packaging — answered.

Do you support new-model launch programs?

Yes. We design and build returnable racks and dunnage to launch timing, and can re-spec existing racks for a line change or model-year update.

Can you build line-side WIP carts and sequencing dollies?

Yes — work-in-process carts, sequencing dollies, and returnable racks are core products, engineered to your line-side flow.

How do you protect Class-A surfaces?

We design custom foam and fabric dunnage to cradle Class-A parts, so surfaces arrive clean trip after trip.

Can you support multi-plant programs?

Yes. One accountable partner builds to the same print across your plants, with repair and managed-program options to keep fleets whole.

Got a part or a launch coming?

Send a print — we’ll turn around a rack concept and a number.
