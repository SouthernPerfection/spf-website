What we service

Keep the fleet you already own running.

Repair

Straighten, re-weld, and rebuild damaged returnable racks and stack racks back to spec.

Refurbishment

Full rack refurbishment — strip, repair, refinish, and return to service like new.

Modification

Re-spec racks for a new part, a line change, or a launch without buying a whole new fleet.

Replacement

Write-off units replaced and matched to your existing fleet, built to the original print.

Why it matters

The steadiest revenue in the business is your uptime.

Repair and refurbishment is recurring by nature — a rack repair service keeps your installed base in service and off the critical path. We’re positioned in the Southeast & Midwest corridor for fast turnaround near your plant, and every repair runs under the same ISO 9001 quality system as our new builds.

Want us to run the whole fleet for you — tracking, inspection, repair, and replacement on a schedule? That’s our managed returnable programs.

FAQ

Rack repair — answered.

What can be repaired versus replaced?

We straighten, re-weld, and rebuild damaged racks back to spec. Units too far gone are replaced and matched to your existing fleet.

What is the turnaround on repairs?

We are positioned in the Southeast and Midwest corridor for fast turnaround near your plant. Send photos of the damage and we will scope it.

Will repaired racks match our existing fleet?

Yes. We repair and build replacements to your original print so the fleet stays consistent.

Do you offer scheduled fleet service?

Yes. Our managed programs add scheduled inspection, pickup, and depot repair across the fleet.

Racks getting beat up?

Send photos of the damage — we’ll scope a repair and a number.
