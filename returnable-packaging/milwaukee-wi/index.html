FAQ

Returnable packaging in Milwaukee — answered.

Do you serve manufacturers in Milwaukee, WI?

Yes. Southern Perfection Fabrication builds returnable packaging in Byron, GA and freight-serves the Milwaukee, WI market — about a day and a half by truck away. We design, weld, and finish every program in-house, then ship it to your plant.

How do you ship returnable racks to Milwaukee?

By LTL or full truckload, roughly 840 miles from our Byron, GA plant. Each rack is engineered to cube out the trailer so you pay to ship parts, not air, and empties nest or fold to cut return freight.

Do you build to our print?

Yes, build-to-print is our standard. Send a drawing, CAD, or a photo with dimensions and we’ll return a concept and a number — usually within a couple of business days.

Can you repair racks we already run?

Yes. We inspect, re-weld, re-member, and re-finish worn returnable racks — even racks we didn’t build — back to spec, usually in 1–2 weeks per batch.

Got a part in Milwaukee that needs a rack?

Send a photo or a print — we’ll come back with a concept and a number.
