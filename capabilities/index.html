Contract metal fabrication

What is contract metal fabrication?

Contract metal fabrication is the outsourced design and manufacture of metal parts and assemblies — cutting, forming, machining, welding, and finishing — built to a customer’s print. A single-source fabricator does all of it under one roof instead of routing the job between separate shops.

Southern Perfection Fabrication is a full-service contract metal fabricator: SolidWorks design, laser and plasma cutting, CNC forming to 230 tons, CNC machining, 30+ welding stations plus FANUC robotic, and in-house finishing — all ISO 9001 and CAGE 2W654.

One roof, one purchase order, one accountable team — see how that cut a manufacturer’s lead time roughly in half.
