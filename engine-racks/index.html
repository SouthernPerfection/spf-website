FAQ

Engine racks — answered.

How much weight can an engine rack hold?

We engineer engine racks to your part weight and load points — from small engines to heavy powertrain assemblies. Send the weight and we'll spec the structure to carry it safely.

Do engine racks protect machined surfaces?

Yes. We integrate foam or fabric cradles and locating features so machined and sealing surfaces are protected and located precisely in transit.

Shipping engines or powertrain?

Send a part or a print — we'll spec a rack and a number.
