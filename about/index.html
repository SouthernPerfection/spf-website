Turning drawings into precision metal since 1982.

Southern Perfection Fabrication is a family-owned, fourth-generation contract metal fabricator in Byron, Georgia — building returnable steel racks, weldments, and material handling since 1982. Designed, cut, formed, machined, welded, and finished under one roof across a 100,000 sq ft plant. ISO 9001 certified, CAGE 2W654.
