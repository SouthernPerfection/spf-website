Custom foam inserts

What are custom foam inserts?

Custom foam inserts are precisely-cut foam cavities that cradle a part inside a rack, tote, or crate so it cannot shift, rub, or drop in transit. Also called protective foam packaging or foam packaging inserts, they pair with a returnable rack to protect finish-critical and fragile parts trip after trip.

Southern Perfection Fabrication designs custom foam packaging around your exact part geometry — routed, die-cut, or laminated from the right foam density for the load. Because we also build the rack and the dunnage, the foam, the nest, and the steel are engineered together as one protective system — not sourced from three vendors.

We supply custom foam inserts for automotive interior and finish-critical parts, EV and battery modules, aerospace and defense components, and general manufacturing — anywhere a part is too valuable to let touch steel.

Foam types & when to use them

Polyethylene (PE)

Firm closed-cell foam that holds up over hundreds of returnable trips — ideal for heavier parts.

Polyurethane (PU)

Softer open-cell foam for delicate, lighter, or finish-critical parts.

Cross-Linked

Fine-celled premium foam for high-value or Class-A surfaces.

ESD / Anti-Static

Conductive foam for electronics, sensors, and battery components.

FAQ

Custom foam inserts — answered.

Do you make custom foam inserts in-house?

Yes. We design and fabricate custom foam inserts to your part geometry, cut and shaped to cradle the part and absorb transit shock.

Are your foam inserts reusable/returnable?

Yes. We build durable, reusable foam dunnage engineered to ride your returnable loop, not one-way packaging.

Can foam inserts pair with our racks?

Yes. We design the foam and the rack together as one system, so parts sit right and arrive clean.

What foam is best for returnable packaging?

For returnable loops, firmer closed-cell foams like polyethylene hold up best over hundreds of trips; softer polyurethane suits delicate or finish-critical parts. We spec the density to your part and cycle.

Do foam inserts work with steel racks?

Yes — that is the point. We design the foam nest and the steel rack together so the part is cradled and protected as one system.

Can you match our exact part shape?

Yes. We rout or die-cut foam to your part geometry from CAD or a physical sample, so each part seats in a defined, cushioned pocket.
